How It Works
01
Find a Song
Go to Find Music and browse beginner collections on Ultimate Guitar — Most Popular, Top Rated, or Absolute Beginner. Or search for any song by name.
02
Download the Song Sheet
On the Ultimate Guitar page, click Download as PDF below the song sheet. It's free — no account needed. A photo or screenshot works just as well.
03
Upload to Vizi
Go to Upload Music and send the PDF or photo. Vizi reads the song sheet and sends your lesson to the fretboard instantly.
04
Fretboard Lights Up
84 LEDs illuminate each chord position as Vizi walks you through the song. Ask questions, request an easier version, or slow down any tricky section.
📂
Already have your own music? You can upload any song sheet you already have — a PDF, a photo of printed sheet music, or just type the chords in yourself. Head to Upload Music and Vizi will take it from there.
